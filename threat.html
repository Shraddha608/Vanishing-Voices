Threats to Endangered Species

Habitat Loss

Destruction of natural habitats due to urbanization, agriculture, and deforestation poses a severe threat to species' survival.

Climate Change

Climate change affects ecosystems and wildlife, causing shifts in habitat ranges, altering food sources, and leading to extinction for some species.

Poaching and Hunting

Illegal poaching and hunting for animal products, like ivory or fur, result in the loss of wildlife populations and species endangerment.

Pollution

Pollutants from industrial and agricultural sources contaminate ecosystems, posing dangers to animal health and disrupting food chains.

Invasive Species

Non-native species introduced into an ecosystem can harm native species, leading to competition for resources and loss of biodiversity.
